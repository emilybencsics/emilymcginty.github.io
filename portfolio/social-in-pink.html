McDonald’s Summer Campaign

A youth-focused campaign designed for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube Shorts. I led strategy, content development, and post-campaign reporting.

Campaign Overview

This social-first campaign aimed to drive mobile app installs and build relevance with Gen Z. It featured influencer partnerships, trend-driven UGC templates, in-store promotions, and limited-time content drops.

Key Results

  • 7.5M total video views
  • +45% engagement across all platforms
  • 12% increase in social followers
  • 23K app installs during the 6-week promo period
