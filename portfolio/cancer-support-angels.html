Campaign Overview

Cancer Support Angels is a not-for-profit organisation supporting individuals affected by cancer across Melbourne, Australia. From July 2021 to March 2023, I co-led the organisation's social media marketing strategy with a focus on community building, brand visibility, and fundraising. The campaign aimed to engage, uplift, and support individuals and families navigating cancer while encouraging donations to fund practical assistance programs.

Through consistent storytelling, community-driven content, and accessible messaging, the campaign helped grow the organisation's online presence, strengthened engagement with supporters, and positioned Facebook as a key channel for both awareness and support.

Campaign Overview

The Facebook campaign was designed to build connection, inspire action, and create a supportive space for people affected by cancer.

I co-led the planning and execution of a consistent content strategy that included monthly Survivor Spotlights, weekly Self Care Saturday posts, and quarterly giveaways. I sourced ideas, wrote content and created visuals and captions in line with the organisation's tone. Campaigns were aligned with awareness dates and key seasonal moments, with donation messaging gently woven into relevant posts.

Content was scheduled and published through Buffer with ongoing monitoring to guide future planning. Each post was designed to be helpful, uplifting, and community-focused, making Facebook a key part of the organisation's broader fundraising and support efforts.

Key Results

The Facebook campaign helped build a warm, engaged online community while encouraging donations and product sales to support those living with cancer.

  • Delivered consistent content across a 20-month period, including 80+ Self Care Saturday posts, 20+ Survivor Spotlights, and 6+ Giveaways
  • Fostered regular interaction and peer support through community-focused content.
  • Raised awareness of the organization's support services and mission across new audiences.
  • Supported fundraising efforts through increased visibility of donation links, blog traffic, and product sales.

While specific metrics were not recorded, qualitative feedback and steady page growth reflected a campaign that prioritized impact, empathy, and connection.

The campaign helped transform Facebook from a static page into an active space for community, care, and conversation, ultimately supporting Cancer Support Angels' mission to ensure no one faces cancer alone.

Creative Highlights

Charity of Choice

This post was chosen to highlight Cancer Support Angels as the charity of choice because it builds credibility, increases trust, and reinforces the organisation's legitimacy to potential donors.

Featuring this recognition on Facebook maximizes reach among supporters and the wider community, helping to drive donations by showing that respected partners have chosen to back the cause.

Self-Care Saturday

Posting Self Care Saturday content is a consistent strategy to engage the community with value-driven, supportive messaging.

These posts encourage ongoing interaction and keep the page relevant beyond donation requests, while also positioning Cancer Support Angels as a source of practical wellbeing advice for patients and carers.

Regular, predictable content also boosts page retention and repeat visits.

Research Opportunity

Sharing relevant research aligns the organisation with evidence-based information and shows that Cancer Support Angels is informed and connected within the cancer community.

Strategically, it builds trust, offers value to followers, and encourages shares, expanding reach to new audiences who may then discover the organisation's mission and support its work.
